# DermaCheck Color Palette

## New Sophisticated Color Scheme

The app now uses a sophisticated, earthy color palette inspired by neutral and muted tones that create a calming, professional medical aesthetic.

### Primary Colors

#### Sage Green Family
- **Primary**: `#6B7C6B` - Medium muted sage green
- **Primary Container**: `#8A9B8A` - Lighter sage green
- **Accent**: `#8A9B8A` - Sage green accent

#### Warm Beige Family
- **Secondary**: `#D4C4A8` - Light warm beige
- **Secondary Container**: `#E8DDC8` - Very light warm beige
- **Accent Container**: `#D4C4A8` - Warm beige accent

### Surface Colors

#### Creamy Neutrals
- **Surface**: `#F7F5F3` - Creamy off-white
- **Surface Variant**: `#E8E4E0` - Light warm beige
- **Background**: `#F2F0ED` - Soft warm background

### Text Colors

#### Charcoal and Taupe
- **On Primary**: `#FFFFFF` - White text on primary
- **On Secondary**: `#4A4A4A` - Dark charcoal
- **On Surface**: `#3A3A3A` - Charcoal gray
- **On Background**: `#3A3A3A` - Charcoal gray
- **On Surface Variant**: `#5A5A5A` - Medium gray
- **On Accent**: `#3A3A3A` - Charcoal text on accent

### Accent Colors

#### Muted Tones
- **Error**: `#B85450` - Muted red
- **On Error**: `#FFFFFF` - White text on error
- **Outline**: `#9A9A9A` - Medium gray
- **Outline Variant**: `#C4C4C4` - Light gray

## Color Usage Guidelines

### Background Hierarchy
1. **Main Background**: `#F2F0ED` - Soft warm background
2. **Card Backgrounds**: `#F7F5F3` - Creamy off-white
3. **Accent Backgrounds**: `#E8DDC8` - Very light warm beige

### Text Hierarchy
1. **Primary Text**: `#3A3A3A` - Charcoal gray (headings, important text)
2. **Secondary Text**: `#5A5A5A` - Medium gray (body text, descriptions)
3. **Tertiary Text**: `#9A9A9A` - Medium gray (subtle text, icons)

### Interactive Elements
1. **Primary Buttons**: `#6B7C6B` - Medium muted sage green
2. **Icons**: `#6B7C6B` - Medium muted sage green
3. **FAB**: `#6B7C6B` - Medium muted sage green

### Accent Areas
1. **Privacy Cards**: `#E8DDC8` - Very light warm beige
2. **Avatar Backgrounds**: `#D4C4A8` - Light warm beige
3. **Activity Icons**: `#D4C4A8` - Light warm beige

## Design Philosophy

### Calming Medical Aesthetic
- **Neutral Base**: Creates a calming, non-intrusive environment
- **Warm Undertones**: Maintains a welcoming, human feel
- **Professional**: Sophisticated enough for medical applications
- **Accessible**: High contrast ratios for readability

### Color Psychology
- **Sage Green**: Associated with health, nature, and healing
- **Warm Beige**: Creates comfort and approachability
- **Charcoal Gray**: Provides stability and professionalism
- **Creamy Whites**: Maintains cleanliness and clarity

## Implementation Notes

### Theme Integration
All colors are defined in `src/theme/theme.js` and automatically applied throughout the app using React Native Paper's theming system.

### Consistency
- All components use the theme colors
- Consistent color usage across all screens
- Proper contrast ratios maintained
- Accessibility standards met

### Future Extensions
The color palette can be easily extended with additional shades while maintaining the overall aesthetic:
- Darker sage greens for emphasis
- Lighter beiges for subtle backgrounds
- Additional gray tones for text hierarchy

## Visual Impact

This color palette creates a:
- **Professional** medical application feel
- **Calming** user experience
- **Modern** and sophisticated appearance
- **Accessible** interface for all users
- **Cohesive** design language throughout the app
